The Opportunity

Traveling is expensive and complicated. With plane tickets costing hundreds of dollars, it can be hard to justify spending money once you are at your destination. Mile plans vary drastically by airline, and not all channels to earn miles are known to the consumer. At a time where all airlines seem to be bleeding into one monopoly, fliers feel frustrated and uninspired to fly. .

Project goals

  • Carry On is a sister application by Alaska Airlines that offers discounted plane and experience bundles to its members. Carry On aims to make vacations less costly overall, but rich in experience. Built in 3 semesters, divided the year into three deliverable sections. The first quarter we focuses on UX research, the second UI app design, and the third covering marketing deliverables. UX research includes qualitative and quantitative research, in-person interviews, SWOT analysis, user personas, empathy maps, user stories, and customer journey maps. UI app design includes two user prototypes as well as brand direction. Marketing materials include digital ads, promotional video, postcards, and setup at portshowlio.

The Process

Mentorship and research:

First, Trevor and I met with mentors over the summer to provide scaffolding for the research part of our deliverables. We sent out three online surveys and crafted the questions for our in-person interviews. We interviewed students downstrias at SCC as well as individuals at the SEA-TAC light rail. Next, we built our four personas, charted their emotions using empathy maps, and followed four separate journeys using customer journey maps. Finally, we built out four user stories per persona and completed our storyboarding.

User interface:

Building out our user interface proved to be challenging. The scope of our project suddenly felt very large, and we needed to hone in on certain tasks flows to complete the amount of work in a given time. We successfully finished two task flows, itinerary and booking an experience.

Marketing:

Now that we had our research and app built, it was time to create marketing collateral for Carry On. We focused on digital ads, print ads, video, and marketing materials like postcards to be distributed at the Portshowlio

The Solution

Carry On by Alaska Airlines gives Alaska Airlines Mileage Club members access to discounted experience offerings at their final destination. In addition, Carry On gives members points on experiences booked through Carry On to use towards their next flight.
