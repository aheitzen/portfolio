The problem

Create an interesting way to view your horoscope using a combination of web development, animation, design, and illustration techniques, all while providing an easy, functional and visually interesting web experience.

Project goals

  • Create a zodiac website that features all 12 zodiac signs in an interesting and engaging way
  • Strengthen illustration skills by drawing 12 zodiac signs and animate them using the BodyMovin plugin
  • Strengthen JavaScript skills by presenting components in a slider format

The Process

Research

First I sourced websites that told stories in a unique way. Then I thought about what content would work best for this project, and settled on the 12 zodiac signs. Being a lifelong skeptic, landing on the zodiac was a surprise to me, but I am so glad I did. Identifying with nature, the elements, and the planets was a grounding experience.

Animation and Illustration:

I illustrated and animated 12 zodiac signs and their associated symbols, which was a great accomplishment coming from someone who is adamantly in the “not an artist” camp. One of the greatest takeaways from the animation process is how painful it is in the beginning, but by your third attempt things move smoothly. Once I had my assets built it was time to dive into the code.

Development:

Bringing my design assets into the browser was a seamless experience using the BodyMovin plugin. I built a home screen, two levels of navigation, a slider for the signs, as well as a nifty about page. Strengthening my JavaScript skills and learning a new library always feels rewarding.

The Solution

Building a beautiful zodiac website while learning more about current animation and web technologies was a joy. Creating a website that is functional and visually unique appeals to people who are interested in astrology and also individuals who appreciate dynamic web technology. Living in a time where technology and art can so seamlessly intertwine is a privilege and I believe this project showcases that relationship.
