The Opportunity

Contraception deserts, counties in the United States where there is less than one public health clinic for every 1,000 women eligible for publicly funded contraception, are expanding in the United States. Reorganization of Title X under the Trump administration will expand these areas, and women who rely on Title X and the Afforadble Care Act disportopionally live in contraception deserts. Women need reliable access to contraception regardless of where they live and the sociopolitical status of their county.

Project goals

  • Provide seamless contraception delivery to women who live in impacted areas
  • Deliverables include logo and design system, personas, empathy maps, user flows, and prototype
  • Provide easily accessible birth control for women who live busy lifestyles

The Process

Research and user experience design

I conducted a series of qualitative interviews asking women about their general experience with birth control. After extracting their pain points I moved into constructing personas and customer journey maps based on my qualitative research.

Task flows and prototyping:

From there I broke down how Choice works by following my two personas flows through the app. Then I built out the onboarding process, while conducting intermittent user testing

Design Deliverables:

Now that the user research and interface were sound I moved onto other design assets that would bolster Choice as a brand.

The Solution

Choice offers women comprehensive contraceptive care. Choice puts the power of women's healthcare back into the hands of women by offering different types of hormonal birth control and 24 hour Choice practitioner consultations. Choice empowers women to take charge of their reproductive health. With birth control delivered and prescribed digitally, access to birth control will no longer be dependent on where you live.
